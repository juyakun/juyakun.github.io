Biography

Yakun Ju (Member of IEEE, ACM, CCF) is currently a Lecturer (Assistant Professor) at the School of Computing and Mathematical Sciences, University of Leicester, closely working with Professor [Huiyu Zhou] . Before that, he served as the Research Fellow at the ROSE Lab , School of Electrical and Electronic Engineering, Nanyang Technological University, working with Professor [Alex Chichung Kot], and the Postdoctoral Fellow at the Department of Electrical and Electronic Engineering, The Hong Kong Polytechnic University, working with Professor [Kin-Man Lam].

He earned the B.E. at the School of Mechanical Engineering, Sichuan University, in 2016, and the Ph.D. at the School of Computer Science and Technology, Ocean University of China, in 2022. In his Ph.D. career, he was supervised by Professor [Junyu Dong]. In 2020 and 2021, he was a visiting Ph.D. student at Peking University under Professor [Yuxin Peng] and The Hong Kong Polytechnic University under Professor [Kin-Man Lam].

His current research interests are mainly 3D reconstruction, medical image processing, underwater vision, computational imaging, and low-level tasks. Dr. Ju serves as a Reviewer for numerous journals/conferences, and as Editorial Board/Guest Editor for 5 journals.
